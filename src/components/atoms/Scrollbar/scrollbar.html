Custom Scrollbar

Internal Scroll Design

Notice how the scrollbar respects the card's curvature. It is physically offset from the edges to prevent "popping out" at the corners.

End of content.

How to Use

Add the custom-scrollbar class to any scrollable container:

<div class="custom-scrollbar overflow-y-auto h-[300px]">
  Your content here...
</div>

States:

  • Default: Grey thumb (grey-secondary)
  • Hover: Green thumb (green-secondary)
  • Active: Dark green thumb (green-primary)
